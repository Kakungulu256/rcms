The manager of a certain apartment plans to install smoke detectors which is essential for fire safety however tenants are worried about how the device works after being told by a scientist that smoke detectors contain a small amount of the radioactive element called Americium-241. The substance emits a certain radiation which triggers the alarm and can be applied in other areas in everyday life see figure 4. Although Americium-241 is radioactive, it is used in very small amounts and safely enclosed though it expires after some time. To ensure long-term safety, scientists monitor how its radioactivity decreases over time see figure 3. Despite the efforts by the scientist, the tenants remained worried about the move by the manager.
